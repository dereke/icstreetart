All the info

ICStreetArt plans to break the mould with its exciting releases from some of the world's top street artists.

Blurring th elines between where art sits in the meta verse and the real world by using the mentality of the graffiti medium to create and destroy at the same time. Each artist's NFT we release on our platform will have a physical release that goes along side it, you can then choose to keep the NFT or destroy it to be sent the physical release, giving you the gamble on what's the golden goose.

As with Graffiti where it's there one day and gone the next, the twist on our releases is that when the next artist has their drop you can choose to trade in another artist's work for their release, just like one artist would paint over another in the real world, why not lay that down in the meta verse?

This option means it would be worth keeping hold of your stock as your favourite artist might drop your dream print on the next release and your only way of owning it is by destroying an NFT. But each NFT destroyed makes each release rarer, so which game do you play?

Like a hall of fame where artists paint their walls, knowing they could last only hours, we hope to bring this same feeling of excitement of not knowing what’s next…

With each drop, you have a window to decide if you keep or burn which then decides the edition of each artwork, guaranteeing you a unique and limited edition no matter if you choose the physical release or NFT. Keep your eyes peeled as we have fun with our online feuds between artists and look forward to collaborations and battles as they fight for virtual wall space. Making you in charge of the size of editions and rarity of stock. Earn or burn!
